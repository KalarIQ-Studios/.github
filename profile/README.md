## 🌟 About Us

**Kalariq Studios** is a **principal-led AI & Cloud Engineering Studio** based in **Surat, India**.

We run live cloud operations, migrations, and cost optimization for production systems — and build AI applications, agents, and computer-vision products end-to-end.

Our focus is on two things:
- **AI Engineering** — applications, agents, and computer vision, shipped to production
- **Cloud Engineering** — operations, migrations, FinOps, and scalable infrastructure for live systems

> We're not a consultancy. We're an engineering studio that builds and runs real systems.

### 🤖 AI Applications & Agents
- Custom AI solutions for real-world business problems
